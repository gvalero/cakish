Story

A modern pavlova, interpreted with a softer kind of luxury.

Cakish does not aim to recreate tradition exactly. Instead, it offers a refined, contemporary interpretation: lighter in mood, more sculptural in presentation, and styled for the kinds of celebrations that feel intimate and memorable.

Modern

Not quite traditional, intentionally so.

The Cakish approach keeps the airy elegance people associate with pavlova while reworking the shape, styling, and occasion into something more contemporary.

Refined

Designed to sit beautifully on the table.

Soft neutrals, floral detailing, and restrained presentation help each piece feel graceful, elevated, and quietly celebratory.

Meaningful

Made for occasions people remember.

From intimate birthdays to family celebrations, each order is positioned as part dessert, part centrepiece, and part gesture.
